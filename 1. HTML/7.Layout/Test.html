About Restro

Street & Straw, located in Madhubani, Bihar, is a dining establishment that offers a variety of cuisines, including Chinese and European dishes.Nestled in a charming setting, this restaurant is a perfect retreat for couples seeking a romantic escape. From the moment you step in, the ambiance sets the tone - soft lighting, elegant decor, and cozy seating create an intimate atmosphere that feels like a private getaway. The attention to detail in the design reflects the restaurant's dedication to offering more than just a meal; it's an experience. The cuisine is nothing short of extraordinary. The menu boasts a thoughtful selection of dishes, each crafted with precision and passion. The flavors are perfectly balanced, and the presentation is an art form. The staff deserves special mention for their impeccable service. Attentive without being intrusive, they ensure that every need is met with grace and efficiency. Their recommendations are spot-on, adding to the overall experience. Whether it's a special occasion or a spontaneous date night, this restaurant delivers on all fronts. The combination of a romantic ambiance, exceptional food, and outstanding service makes it a must-visit destination. It's more than just dining; it's a memory waiting to be made.
